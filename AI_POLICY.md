# AI Usage Policy

resurrect.wezterm welcomes AI-assisted contributions, however there is an expectation.

## Requirement: understand your contribution

You must be able to explain every change you submit, including what it does and how it interacts with the 
rest of the plugin, without relying on an AI tool to do so. AI is an acceptable means of producing a change; 
comprehension of that change is a requirement for submitting it.

## AI disclosure

Disclosing AI usage is optional. You may note the tool used and the extent of AI assistance if you wish.

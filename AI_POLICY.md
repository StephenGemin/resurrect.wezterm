# AI Usage Policy

resurrect.wezterm welcomes AI-assisted contributions. This policy is not intended to discourage
that. It sets a single expectation for all contributions, regardless of how they were produced.

## Requirement: understand your contribution

You must be able to explain every change you submit, including what it does and
how it interacts with the rest of the plugin, without relying on an AI tool to do so.
AI is an acceptable means of producing a change; comprehension of that change is
a requirement for submitting it.

## AI disclosure

Disclosing AI usage is optional. You may note the tool used and the extent of AI assistance if you wish.

## On unvetted contributions ("slop")

Slop is output that looks plausible enough to pass a glance but that the author never actually vetted. 
This project does not maintain a denouncement list and will not publicly identify contributors. 
However, contributions that are clearly unvetted will most likely not be merged. This includes code the author cannot
explain, or changes that place the burden of validation on the maintainer. The standard
applied is not whether a human wrote the code, but **whether a human understands it**.
